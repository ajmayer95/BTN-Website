Yolk Sac

Embryonic vascular flows & pulsatility
← Back to Research

We study heartbeat-driven flow in the embryonic yolk sac and how pulsatile forcing shapes vascular development. From time-lapse microscopy, we extract vessel centerlines, construct attributed graphs, and infer time-varying flow. These measurements let us quantify pulsatility and heterogeneity across developmental stages and embryos. The goal is to connect observed dynamics to physics-based models of network adaptation.
