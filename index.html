Flows, networks, and adaptation across biology

We study how living transport networks — from embryonic vasculature to fungal hyphae — form, adapt, and function under dynamic conditions. Our group develops physics-based theory, algorithms, and open-source tools to extract, analyze, and model network flows from images and time-series data.
